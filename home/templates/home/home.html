General Knowledge (GK)

In Nepal, General Knowledge (GK) plays a crucial role in the preparation for the Lok Sewa (Public Service) exams. These exams are the gateway to securing government positions in various departments, and having a strong grasp of GK is essential for success. The GK section of the exam tests a candidate's knowledge in a wide range of topics, including Nepal's history, geography, politics, economy, and current affairs. A deep understanding of Nepal’s social, cultural, and political landscape, along with global awareness, is vital for aspirants aiming to serve the nation. Regular study and staying updated with national developments can greatly improve one’s chances of excelling in the Lok Sewa exams.
